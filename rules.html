Server Rules

1. Do not spam

Refrain from sending excessive messages or content that may disrupt the gameplay experience.

2. No Racial Slurs

Do not use racial slurs or any other discriminatory language.

3. Do Not beg for free stuff from admins

Do not request or beg for free items, resources, or advantages from server administrators.

4. Base Building

No more than one base per tribe

5. Do Not grief other players

Do not destroy or damage other players' structures or belongings without their explicit permission.

6. Do not ask to be staff you can apply like everyone else!

Staff positions are filled through an application process open to all players in the discord server

7. Submit a Ticket if you plan to buy stuff from admins

Before purchasing any items from server administrators, please submit a ticket to the support team.

8. No human name!

Must use a unique and appropriate username. Any names pertaining the name 'human' will be wiped after a warning.

9. No more than 20 dino's out at a time

Players are limited to having no more than 20 dinosaurs out at any given time.

10. No offline breeding

Do not breed dinosaurs while offline, as this can lead to cluster issues and lag.

11. No building in areas that only spawn specific dinos. We don't want to despawn dinos.

Do not build in areas that are designated for specific dinosaur spawns, as this can lead to the despawning of those creatures.

12. No poaching from other cluster servers. If they ask that is one thing but don't advertise in other clusters.

Do not attempt to poach players from other cluster servers, as this can lead to conflicts and unfair advantages.

13. No asking for Tames that are not tameable in Vanilla.

Do not request tames that are not available in the vanilla game mode.

14. DO NOT MESS WITH ANYTHING IN THE ADMIN BASE! VIOLATORS WILL BE INSTABANNED NO EXCEPTIONS!

Messing with the admin base will result in an instant ban.
